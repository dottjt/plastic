I thought I might take the time to reflect on the past 8 months of my life, as it’s been an incredibly turbulent foray into business, entrepreneurship and coding.

In order to do this, there’s one very important fact you have to understand about me.

I used to be an obsessive porn addict.

I don’t shy away from the fact. I don’t really care what others think about it. It’s simply an aspect of my past which dominated a large portion of my life, and which is a huge, unspoken problem in society today.

This is based on the thousands of people I’ve talked to over the past 8 months, not to mention based on my own friendships and even colleagues who vocally express the daily usage of porn as something ‘normal’.

And hey, I’m totally cool with that.

Perhaps it can help to understand that I’m the kind of person who wakes up at 5:30am each day in order to study, and who takes cold showers, eats Paleo, intermittent fasts, weight lifts etc.

Of course, I simply couldn’t stop. I mean, it’s something that I’d been doing since I was 11 years old. It was a behaviour that had literally been hardwired into my brain from the very moment I could masturbate.

And I absolutely hated it. I hated the guilt of hiding my habit from my partner, and the way it made me view women. I hated the feeling of dependence and helplessness it created. I hated every single thing about it.

But then one day it all changed.

Through much attrition, a former colleague finally convinced me to try meditation and like a series of lightbulbs shattering into the distance, it finally all made sense.

For the first time in my life, I’d discovered a way to sustainably develop control over my mind.

And I knew within that moment of clarity, that I would never masturbate or watch porn ever again and as of writing this today, it’s been more than 300+ days of effortless not doing that.

A few months following this discovery, I decided to create to create a platform to help people explore these same ideas in order to help them overcome their own porn addiction.

You can learn more about it at https://neverfapdeluxe.com/

Now, while I could outline what I managed to achieve in terms of the hundreds of articles I wrote, or how I managed to build an extensive online network to assist more than 700+ porn addicts.

I think it will be more meaningful to share what I personally learnt and discovered throughout this process.

  • I learnt how to build a scalable content API that could reliably serve JSON content to websites, mobile applications and even be exported to ePub and PDF format!
  • I learnt so much about how to build large Elixir Applications including an extensive comments system, an email subscription system, a payment platform and so much more.
  • I learnt how to build and deploy mobile applications, in both React Native and full native Android. Not to mention, I developed my first Chrome application as well.
  • I learnt how to effectively market and promote an online business, gaining more than 900+ members as part of r/NeverFapDeluxe and developing tens of thousands of page views as a result.
  • I learnt how to develop effective content and SEO strategies, in addition to social media content strategies.
  • I learnt and developed new ways to meditate to assist those who struggle with meditation via the Hovering Meditation Method.
  • I learnt how to record, script and produce effective guided meditations. I also recorded a bi-weekly podcast as well.
  • I learnt how to build an extensive accountability and chatbot system to assist people with the porn addiction recovery process.
  • I learnt the value of consistent, hard work as an irreplaceable aspect of success.
  • I learnt how to curate, develop and moderate vibrant communities of individuals. I mean, you wouldn’t believe the amount of drama I had to deal with.
  • I learnt how to design logos and create brand guidelines and everything!

And that really only covers a fraction of the day-to-day of running and building NeverFap Deluxe. For more extensive coverage you can always read the weekly reports on the NeverFap Deluxe Patreon.

Of course, all of the above is truly inconsequential to what I primarily learnt.

Specifically, that effective mental health is about learning to respect balance under all circumstances.

And I’m happy to say that I’ve never been more balanced in my life, and it’s the most wonderful feeling in the world.

So what happened? Why am I writing this post?

Well, I’ve decided to put a Hiatus on NeverFap Deluxe in order to pursue other endeavours. It’s a complicated story, and you can read more about it on my latest Patreon post.

Regardless, as a result of my efforts on NeverFap Deluxe, I have decided to pursue Machine Learning and Data Science instead!

I had a bit of an epiphany in regards to how best I can help porn addicts and I believe the answer lies in Machine Learning and Data Science applications. I have a lot of different ideas, but I believe it will be much more fruitful than a simple information website.

Anyway, it’s been a fantastic journey to say the least, and the learnings have been considerable.

Furthermore, I have some fantastic templates and processes in place for my next big endeavour, so it truly has set me up for success later down the line.
