I don’t feel like doing anything today.

I don’t feel like eating. I don’t feel like writing. I don’t feel like seeing. Quite frankly, if I could retract all sentiment and savagely burn it under the guise of environmental distaste, I’d have become leader of the Greens by now. Shove ye’ bucket into me you cluck imitation of brown heart. Bore down on ye’, bore down on ye’ foresee how he be we. 

I want to be sad today. I very rarely get the chance to be sad anymore and just sit in thoughtlessness, hovering at the mismatch of white and grey colluding above. I want my heart to rip itself out of its socket and tell stories of broken jurisdiction, lamenting itself like a frozen river over the feelings trying to find warmth amongst the permafrost. It’s almost sexy, if I weren’t so deranged. 

Instead, what you usually get is a machine with a killer instinct. A hunter with numerous guns ranging from the deadly to the silent. Oh, and he’s a quick learner too. You want something learnt? Well do it yourself, because he ain’t got time to help you with your incompetence. He has a lovely girlfriend as well. Who’d thunk it. The daring child and his curvy babe. 

I feel somewhat robbed of my sanity.
