I never intended to be meaningful.

Meaning is one of those funny, double-speak words. I’ve noticed that meaning is not an individual construct, so much as it is about what other people find meaning in.

It helps to compare meaning like a wave. Some people are at the top of that wave, riding this so called meaningful paradigm, while others of us.

Yet we are all still part of the same ocean, as small droplets.

This reflects the relative, social paradigm that has inherited this world.

I personally believe there is almost too much meaning in this world. Too much
