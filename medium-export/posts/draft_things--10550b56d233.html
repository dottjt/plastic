Here are a few things you should probably know about me:

  • I’m a button pusher. I push buttons. It’s what I do. It’s what my brain wants and does best, and like a map of stars laid out on a flat universe, I understand which buttons are important, as well as how they should be pressed.
  • I tend to push the buttons I shouldn’t touch. I can barely help myself.
  • I’m very good with others and while I know how to be that incredibly charismatic extrovert, I don’t particularly care about them. I think it’s because I have other priorities in life. 
  • In other words, I’m self-centred. I want to have three children and that’s probably why I don’t talk to you, because children cost a lot of money and that’s what I’m saving up for. In other words, I’m anti-social beyond repair.
  • I think people are smart. 
  • I don’t know if you would call it a fetish, however I love this idea of being broken into a thousand different pieces and becoming fluid. Maybe it’s because I took a lot of acid in the day. 
  • I’m mentally ill and I have depression. You’ll very rarely see it because I manage and look after myself like a charm, however ideally I’d be on a drug bender right now if it weren’t for human responsibility. 
  • I don’t follow instructions very well.
  • I’m the most self-determined person you’ll ever meet. I take cold showers everyday. I push myself until the point of collapse. 
  • I don’t have many friends. 
  • I’ll do absolutely anything to get ahead, except if it goes against my integrity, which is why I never get ahead. 
  • I’m non-violent. 
  • I talk myself out of everything. I assume it’s worked, because I’ve yet to have my head punched in. 


  • You’ll almost always catch me on my computer.
  • I devour knowledge. 


  • I don’t give into peer pressure. At all. Which means you’ll probably hate me.
