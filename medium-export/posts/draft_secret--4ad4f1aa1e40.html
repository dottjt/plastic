I have a deep dark secret that I would like to share with you today.

Although I ponder as someone who’s a technical geek who loves tinkering with bits and bobs; a keen curator of the electrical arts, this is actually a facade to what truly drives me as a human being. The ancient core whirring within my brittle, infallible structure. 

Which is to say that the all-alluring secret I would like to share with you today is that I should be working in sales. 

Not as a developer.  

Although I’ve only briefly worked in sales in a professional capacity (and it was an absolutely miserable experience, although that’s a story for another time), I’ve been selling stuff all my life and it’s something I love doing. 

In particular, the aspect of sales I love most is trading. The transaction side of things. Which means if we see a garage sale sign on the side of the road, I will forcefully prompt you. 

I think it’s because I’m a really good middleman when it comes to identifying arbitrage. Hell, maybe that’s not even a special talent within itself. Perhaps it’s simply a matter of being in the right time at the right place, and it would be foolish to think otherwise.
