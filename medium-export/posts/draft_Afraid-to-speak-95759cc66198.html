I know, I know, you were probably expecting some kind of granular detour into the shrieking anatomy of my mind, how my brain motions like a traversing scar along the frail guidelines society explodes into your face, but it’s really not about that.

There’s nothing glamourous, nor beautiful about my condition. Sure, every now and then you find yourself stringing together a bunch of words that look snazzy, but the underlying pain is hardly full of cheer and love. I hate it and it extends to hating myself.
