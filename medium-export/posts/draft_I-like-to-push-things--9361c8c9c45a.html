I like to push things.

For some reason, when I think about this aspect of my personality, it immediately brings me back to when I was nine years old. We had to give a gift as part of the Kris Kringle and knowing having been selected to provide a gift for a girl, thought it would be funny if I got her a plastic spider that I’d placed in a tin.

I knew I was going to get in trouble, yet at the same time, it simply was something I had to do. It’s not that I needed to be taught a lesson, I simply interested in what that lesson might be.

I think it also explains why I get into so many awkward situations. I just don’t care enough to mitigate the consequence. In fact, I’m interested in what that consequence might be.
