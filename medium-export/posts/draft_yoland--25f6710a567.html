I can’t say I like how I feel right now. My face carries an eerie tinge of melodramatic disgrace, like a fat pig lickin’ his fingers for a crumb or two. We ain’t gottem. Flutter down to the mart where we’ll keep em’ lazy, cause Dallas is just another chronological heartbreak waitin’ to happen. Everything feels foreign and I’m not sure I appreciate it. 

This post was supposed to be an update on my life, but the timing has co-incided with the agonising insecurity in me. Fake acceptance, dripping down the walls with a splash and a demonstratively undecided hokey. Children guzzled in illegal glitter. The posterior screams postulate cum; tangled in a dizzy mess of chorded white and interconnected misery. 

Here is what is on the menu:

  • I’ve really been getting into guitar. Some technical stuff. Largely, messing around with chords and sounds to see what tickles my fancy. Who knows how long it will last. 
  • Physics. I’ve been consuming as much as I can. I can. The whole process has been interesting and straight forward. I really look forward to exploring this further. Thankfully. My main reasoning for studying physics is that I believe it will really help with my maths. 
  • Maths. It’s been a pleasure hating you. I’ve tried read a few books but it’s been making no sense to me. I think it’s because I need to properly learn mathematical induction first before I can move on. 
  • Coding. Actually, I haven’t been coding that much lately, except for stuff at work.
