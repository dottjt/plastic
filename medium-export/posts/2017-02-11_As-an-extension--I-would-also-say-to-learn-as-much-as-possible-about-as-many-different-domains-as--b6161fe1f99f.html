As an extension, I would also say to learn as much as possible about as many different domains as possible. Assembly language, compilers, text editors, functional programming, transducers etc. Because ultimately, that’s what’s going to make you more knowledgeable in an interview.

Not to mention, being able to spiel on for hours on end about the 1’s and 0’s that make you tick demonstrates passion, and if you really love code, it’s something that will come naturally with your growing curiosity into how computers work.
