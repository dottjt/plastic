Today has been a bit of a struggle, so I thought I might write it out. 

First, I should be proud of who I am, because I have not struggled like this in a long time. In fact, I have been consistently productive and focused on my goals and who I want to be. 

I’ve decided to leave my PR & Communications position after nearly one year and it’s been emotionally… well, it’s somewhat of a paradigm shift for me. I don’t feel worried or afraid of the future. I think there are beautiful things to come, however there’s also a lot of adjusting that needs to take place if it is going to be a smooth transition.

Earlier I was contemplating my vices and while I did partially act out on them, I didn’t do anything significant. The biggest adjustment is with how I perceive and work with my available time. I no longer have a rigid schedule that binds me to my purpose, which means I need to be much more careful with how I plan and spend my time. 

Today is a bit special in that it’s a public holiday, so it’s like a trial, I guess. At least I now understand my expectations, which can only be a good thing. 

Also, the frustration of programming is something which is affecting me, but thankfully it’s only temporary and something which I can work with.
