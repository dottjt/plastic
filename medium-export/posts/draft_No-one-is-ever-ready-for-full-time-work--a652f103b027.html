Why are millennials so adverse to working full-time?

I’m not entirely sure why life is so incredibly difficult as millennial, however it just damn-well is.

To me it’s not about the fact that we’ve been priced out of owning anything or the complete lack of career opportunities that were once available to generation’s past. In a way, I see those described limitations as a challenge to allow the best and brightest to push themselves forward and exceed with profound clarity.

Arguably, the most wanky thing I have ever written. Well, at least as today is concerned.

Given more perspective however, at least in trying to integrate seemingly into a “normal” almost heinously traditional life, I think I have come to respect that it’s not because I’m a millennial, rather, that it’s fucking difficult for everyone.

I put off working for a very long time. It wasn’t until I was 19 when I got my first casual position and even then, I hated working more than 10 times a week.

I viewed work as a necessary evil. That thing we are forced into doing.
