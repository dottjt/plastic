Depression is a response. 

No different to being in denial, or grabbing another donut off the shelf — depression is one of the many things your brain can do when faced with bullshit.

Everyone deals with bullshit in different ways.

Some people like to get really angry and self-justified.

Others pretend like everything is okay, only to crumble. 

Some people say that depression is beautiful, but they clearly have no idea about. 


 

Depression doesn’t make me any different. It’s merely my response to the bullshit we all have to deal with. 

You see, I don’t have any vices. 




There’s a hurt in my heart.


I’m a really tough sell.

And I hope you can appreciate that.

I’m not the kindest person around, though perhaps you’re a lot like me. Maybe you’re not the kindest person either, and that’s what defines you and carries you into your daily life experience. 


But you probably ain’t either. You’re probably just like me, a desperate young person -
