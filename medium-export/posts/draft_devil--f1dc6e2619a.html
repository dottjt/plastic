devil. 

No one likes to be told to think or feel. We find it rude and intrusive, as if to suggest we are incapable of creating our own thoughts and emotions. With a natural emenence, we almost expect our dreams and nightmares to flow from the distraught art spilled in a flurry of dry ink upon the canvas of our minds. 


Just imagine if you were to sit there alone, pretending it were some kind of hollow exchange with the devil. 

Cute, we both know. 

Now this devil, she knows everything about you. Stunning, too. If anything, she’s sexualised on purpose. For some reason she’s wearing lingerie today, which is uncanny because I remember her wearing a bikini last time… anyway, she knows your worst fears as well as your happiest moments, beaming from a fucking smile you vaguely recollect before high-school. Oh, I’m sorry. Did the devil hurt you there? Well, that’s just what the devil wants you to think.
