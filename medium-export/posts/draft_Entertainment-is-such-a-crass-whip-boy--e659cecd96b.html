Entertainment is such a crass whip-boy.

We’re all a bunch of flaccid donkeys, chock-a-block with the emulsified hope our minds smear across the brass incomprehension.

It’s failure on a whole new level and it really hurts; shattered glass on my broken foot, stepping stones for tears in no place 

I mean, all you have to do is stick a coin in the hole and watch the whole hole fall apart into a miserable complex of dots and circles.

The dots are for happiness. The circles, those fucking things, are just bigger dots with holes in them. It etchs like a certain taste that burns bright into fire, tearing the very pigment from my throat.

Shove me inside a four wheeler; fill me up with circles and lines, and smash that brick wall onto the pedal until the complex delineates and cries with laughter. 

My eyes are bleeding for the excitement that comes along with the mystical tyranny of forethought, spiraling into a kaleidoscope of vivid lawlessness.

Casinos are for the money. You want money, don’t you? Theme parks are even better, because they suck you dry and scream ripe into your lungs. The livid, sacrifice taking chunks from your haphazard memories. 

The pain precipitates like a slow dying light onto a flat canvas of cream; a clear indication of the “everyday” man, flaunty daunt. There’s a night lodged down in my arteries and the happiness emanating from my veins are far-fetched.

Oh, those children are just splendid with their new toys. Sarah is asleep from her toy and they all lay their silently, absorbed in the false exterior cradling their souls.

Put the gun down, lackey. It may be loaded, but it’s a helluva lot more fun when you point it at others. Shoot for the prize son! Shoot like you have nothing to lose.

Eventually however, it all just fades away. We look into the eyes of our loved ones and we pretend with a crank silence, as if occupied by a mind we have no hope of understanding.

To retain is to fabricate. To echo is to absolve.
