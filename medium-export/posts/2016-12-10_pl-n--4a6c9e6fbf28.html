Oh please, you cul-de-sac of shit. Sit down and smell the roses. Every sangle won of them, you foward-fuck.

I don’t like my brain and my brain don’t like me. You know, it’s sort of a refreshing tease to be able to write with a veneer of tarnish and convex. I don’t often get the opportunity to express myself often, because often there is often nothing too often to often express at all very often.

Instead, I am sitting at my desk.

Somewhat robotic like. Reading, usually. Consuming informaiton. Building the information highway. Recruiting. Like a really efficient humpty-dump. Learning. Pruning. Distracting. Spiders everywhere, spinning their web around my fuckin’ I don’t know wat you call it.

I don’t quite know if I regret the path I’ve taken. Certainly, it will help me get what I want, which should be good for the kids. Gotta love the kids — those invisible things that don’t quite exist yet, yet somehow extrude this enormous influence on my actions (and reducers HA HA, redux tard). \

Plan. I have a plan. Well, it’s been my plan for a while now and I would say it’s a pretty good one, if I may.

Gonna become a programmer. Single Page Applications. Gonna get paid. Gonna become an internet person. Disappear completely. Detach myself from the fabric and stain, stain, stain.

Y ou want my stain? You gotta pay.

But you know, today is like… my break period. Where I break my emotions and spread them across a series of words, forming sentences, luxurious golden, to maintain this stupid fucking head awake. Anyway, the plan. The plan is all about growth. SOmething. I don’t fuckign know.

I’m really, really, really, really fucking focused, usually most of the time. I wake up at 6am and I study. Man, it’s all I actually do. I study. I read (study reading, of course). Have I broken down? Am I actually a broken man? Someone please tell me.

Yes.

I spend a lot of time building my application and it’s absolutely terrifying. I have literally zero fucking idea what I’m doing, and the more I get done, the more lost I become and the more it becomes this intricate game of survival between me, myself and the insanity that pursues.

The arrogance of thinking it’s possible. That’s what gets you through it. So I’m going to get through it. I feel like I’m nowhere near where I need to be, but at the same time I feel like I’m much farther along than most people. Someone please explain! There’s a rabid donkey attacking me1!!.

Oh, and have you seen my body lately? It looks great. I didn’t know people could look this great. I have pecks, but they sort of look fake breasts. I’m going to have to consult my surgeon about that. It’s a lot of hard work. A lot of forcing yourself to buckle down and get clean.

I have to. I don’t know why, but I have to. Otherwise my kids will hate me, and realise what a fucking sham I am. Wow.

Fuck, this sucks. Writing sucks. It’s like I want to be held, or something. Blind fold me and drug fuck me. You can’t burn what you can’t touch. So touch me. Please.

It’ll pass.
