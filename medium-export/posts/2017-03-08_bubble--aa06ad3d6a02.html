Let me tell you bout’ the bubble.

The bubble is… well… we all know the bubble is a perfect place.

The bubble doesn’t neeeeeed to be intoxicating in order to be sanitary. It’s the kind of thing you’d be happy to feed to your children, only to find them smiling on the carpet with a clove of dishwashing gel smeared across their PLAYFULLY BASHING THMSELVES INTO A DULLED START OF PRE-PACKAED EUPHOIA.

Listen Jebediah, the children are happy and the point is, you should be too.

I suppose you could say that the bubble is a skill, which largely means it’s a choice you consciously make in your own mind, sort of like having sex with your best friend or molesting a bag of candy with your grubby, insoluble hands.

You make the <insert-state-of-mind> decision to join the bubble and become at peace with the bubble, and certainly it’s a decision you learn to become comfortable with — cause we ain’t afraid of reality, you dumb tripe, and the sass in this room has absolutely quadrupled since your arrival.

Oh, silly you.

And by gosh, I really do like the way you’ve brushed your hair today, cause it’s like you actually gave a shit to look your best, and that’s a pretty cool trait, wouldn’t ya say?

Listen, I love the bubble, because when you stop to think about it…

Stop.

Seriously, stop.

Then stop again.

Don’t think about it. Don’t even move.

There’s a clear contradiction standing right behind you and it’s holding a gun to your head and seriously, I know I’m talking way too quickly, but just let it blow your brains out all over the wall and carpets because mum loves you and you’ve got piss running down your leg and it’s absolutely hilarious.

Here’s the spiel: When you…

Oh wait, you may not notice this, as I haven’t yet told you, however please let the author finish because he has immense self-esteem issues and his wife is a fat whore, who is currently attempting to squander his riches.

You know, the kind of riches that my poor working hands have endured. Anyway, the contradiction is quite serious.

Okay, seriously. I will hit you.

I AM VIOLENT. I AM AN IRRATIONAL MAN and like a donkey packed to the rim with steroids and explosives, I will combust at any given moment and take your children down too.

Do you FEEL that? The milky exterior of the bubble, that fucking immaculate excuse of enforced confinement, taunting you and ripping you apart, and into your thoughts. Telling you lies, that you’re stupid, that you’re smart, that you don’t have it, that you’re king WELL I CAN’T FUCKING TAKE IT ANYMORE AND THROW IT ALL AWAY MISTER CAUSE THE PLASTIC WILL SMEAR

Interesting.

The bubble, that white-wash of pre-conceived expectation laundered ever-so beautifully through reality, as if time were a gateway to our most intimate desires, is nothing more than a prejudice we pretend to distort and ignore, as if to say that the whole charade was a simple illusion, that we thought cared more about US than we did IT.

Like the fools before you, there is absolutely nothing to hide. Cause a-one, and a-two, and a-three. four. five!

Six. Seven.

Eight? Nine.

What most people don’t realise is that the bubble cannot co-exist with choice. So really, you’re either making good decisions or shitty decisions.

Nothing less. Nothing more.
