The Reality Of Being A Man

I don’t really know how I feel as a man. 

The sad reality of the 21st century is that men are not taught to feel unique for their gender, in-spite of the fact that my gender is responsible for a large pool of my talents. 

To take things further, men have never really been taught how to feel, which is a problem that the current state of media and press have exacerbated significantly. 

At times I can be an ambitious, driven and confident individual, though these certainly are not just qualities of man, they are qualities of women too.

See what just happened there? See how hard it is just to embrace myself as a man, without having to passively apologise for who I am as an individual? There’s this great shame I almost carry as a man, simply because it is what I’m told on a daily basis through the media. 

The issue is rather simple, in my eyes. I don’t believe there is anything wrong with men. Men are beautiful creatures, destined to create and strive and do amazing things in the world. 

The issue is in the things that we’re told we should not be and this pessimism which has dire consequences in the development of who we are as men. 

Men are told not to rape women. Men are told not to assault women. Men are told not to be assholes. Men are told so many things that were never qualities of man, yet have been branded as an integral part of who we are. 

On the contrary, men are never told how to be good men. Instead, we’re given a list of requirements that have made us afraid to express even the most tattered state of innocence holding us together inside.

As a result, men have become insecure. We loath ourselves over internet pornography and video games, cause who even gives a shit any more. Apparently lounging around as a complete waste on the couch can’t draw any criticism. 

Though the issue is much deeper. 

I believe what is happening in society is that the very idea of man itself is becoming obscured. There seems to be a hatred of tradition from both sides of the camp. Women are sick of being portrayed as weak, and men are sick of having to give a shit. 

These aspects are fair enough, however the problem is that human is inherently a creature of a tradition, rather than rational thought and the moment you remove tradition, you end up with chaos. 

You end up with men who become addicted to porn, men who stop even wanting be in a relationship. You end up with women who begin to despise men for being 

This is the intellectualisation of society which we are seeing more and more each day. 


Individuality in men has completely dissolved into something incomprehensible and now there is nothing. 

Instead, it has been replaced by stupid memes perpetrated by stereotypes. Men like cars. Men like and given the general stupidity of the human race, men around the world are buying it left, right and centre. 

And you know the funny thing? The same shit is happening to women as a result. 

Women are dumb. Women are incapable. Women like make up and not only is it frustrating men because it’s happening, however it’s frustrating the hell out of women as well.
