The importance of relationships

I always knew I needed a girlfriend.

It just made sense in my mind and I knew that if I had a girlfriend, I would be a lot happier.

Of course, eventually that notion turned into insecurity, yet somewhere along the line I got lucky and found a girl. 

Well, the first two girls didn’t work out and yet, I knew that it was still something I needed if I were to get better. 

Relationships are important for a number of reasons. It doesn’t have to be with someone you want to spend the rest of your life with. It can just be a friend. A person that you know. 

Someone that you trust.

I knew that if I had a girlfriend, I could have someone to trust, someone to work my problems through; a kind of therapist who knew every single thing about me. She would hold me accountable and encourage me and inspire me.
